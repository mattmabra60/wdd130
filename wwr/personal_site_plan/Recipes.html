German Cold Cut Sandwich

Estimated Prep Time: 10 minutes

Necessary Ingredients: Rye Bread, Black Forest Ham, Smoked Turkey Breast, Emmental Cheese, Sauerkraut, German Mustard, Pickles, Butter, Salt, Pepper

Description: Experience the flavors of Germany with this hearty cold cut sandwich for an authentic taste of Bavaria!
- Spread butter on one side of each slice of rye bread
- Layer Black Forest ham and smoked turkey breast on one slice of bread
- Top with slices of Emmental cheese and a generous amount of sauerkraut, salt, and pepper
- Spread German mustard on the other slice of bread and place it on top of the sandwich
- add toothpicks to keep the sandwich together and cut the sandwich in half diagonally
- Serve with a side of pickles and enjoy your German-inspired cold cut sandwich!

Spaghetti Carbonara

Estimated Cook Time: 30 minutes

Necessary Ingredients: Spaghetti, Marinara, Eggs, Pancetta, Parmesan Cheese, Black Pepper

Description: A creamy rich cousin of the classic Spaghetti and Red-sauce this is a classic Italian pasta dish for a comfy day indoors.
- Bring a pot of water to a boil
- Cook spaghetti al dente drain and set aside a quarter cup of pasta water
- Crisp up pancetta in a meduim hot pan
- Mix beaten egg yolk with grated cheese in a small bowl
- Add the drained pasta noodles, and marinara to pan with cooked pancetta
- While contantly stirring add egg and cheese mixture until sauce thickens
- Finish by adding as much pasta water as needed to get desired thickness
and garnish with a generous sprinkle of black pepper.

Chicken Curry

Estimated Cook Time: 45 minutes

Necessary Ingredients: Chicken, Curry Paste, Coconut Milk, Onion, Garlic, Ginger, Tomato, Spices

Description: With a striking orange yellow sauce this chicken curry over rice is sure to impress.
- Chop chicken breast into quarter size chunks
- Bring a cup of water and cup of coconut milk to a low simmer
- After the water comes to a slight boil add curry paste, chopped onion, ginger, garlic, tomato
for spices add cumin, turmeric, salt, pepper, and cloves in equal parts
- Let simmer for 20 minutes stirring occasionally
- Rinse a cup of rise under cold water and add 1 and 1/4 cup water and bring to a boil
- Once boiling cover with a lid and reduce heat to low
- While the rice and sauce are simmering brown chicken over high heat in a large skillet
- Finally add cooked chicken to creamy curry suace and let simmer for 5 more minutes
- Serve hot with steamed rice for a hearty meal!

Caesar Salad

Estimated Cook Time: 15 minutes

Necessary Ingredients: Romaine Lettuce, Croutons, Parmesan Cheese, Caesar Dressing, Lemon, Bacon Bits (optional)

Description: Enjoy a crisp and refreshing Caesar salad bound to boost your energy!

Beef Tacos

Estimated Cook Time: 25 minutes

Necessary Ingredients: Ground Beef, Taco Seasoning, Tortillas, White Onion, Cilantro, Limes

Description: Spice up your mealtime with these simple yet flavorful beef street tacos.
- Over medium-high heat brown and add seasoning to ground beef
- Dice white onion and cilantro and then add them to a bowl with a splash of lime juice
- Warm tortillas in the microwave and then cover with tin foil to retain the heat
- Fill warmed tortillas with beef, onion and cilantro mix, garnish with
a squeeze of lime juice, then enjoy with a cold beverage!

Vegetable and Chicken Stir Fry

Estimated Cook Time: 20 minutes

Necessary Ingredients: Chicken Breast, Assorted Vegetables (such as red cabbage, carrots, and snow peas), Soy Sauce, Hoisin Sauce, Garlic, Ginger, Sesame Oil, Rice

Description: Whip up a quick and nutritious vegetable stir fry packed with colorful veggies and tossed in a savory soy sauce-based sauce.
- Dice bell peppers, broccoli, and carrots to desired indivudual sizes
(tip: strips for peppers, bite size for broccoli, and thin spears for carrot)
- Bring a large pan to meduim heat and add seamame oil, garlic, and ginger cook until fragrent
- Add all veggies including snow peas, and allow them to slightly brown along with chopped chicken breast
- Once browned add 1/4 cup water soy sauce and hoisin sauce to taste (equal parts should do the trick)
- Serve over steamed rice for a satisfying and wholesome meal!

Classic Cheeseburger

Estimated Cook Time: 20 minutes

Necessary Ingredients: Ground Beef, Burger Buns, American Cheese, Onion, Pickles, Ketchup, Mustard

Description: Indulge in the timeless appeal of a juicy cheeseburger.
- Thaw and form ground beef into patties approx. 4 inched across and 1 inch in height
- Season with pepper and salt and a small amount of red pepper flakes
(tip: to add extra moisture mix in about a quarter cup of mayo for every pound of beef)
- Bring a meduim size pan to high heat and add a high temp oil like avocado oil
- Sear seasoned beef patties till brown adding a fair amount of pressure while searing
- Stack on a soft bun with Kraft singles american cheese, and all your favorite toppings.

Creamy Gordon Ramsay Eggs

Estimated Cook Time: 10 minutes

Necessary Ingredients: Eggs, Butter, Crème Fraîche, Chives, Salt, Pepper

Description: Indulge in the velvety goodness of Gordon Ramsay's signature creamy scrambled eggs. Perfect for breakfast or brunch, these eggs are cooked low and slow with butter and crème fraîche until beautifully soft and creamy. Garnish with fresh chives and season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve on toast for a luxurious start to your day!
- In a non-stick pan, melt butter over medium-low heat
- Crack eggs into a bowl and whisk until well combined
- Pour eggs into the pan and let them sit for a few seconds
- Using a rubber spatula, gently stir the eggs in a figure-eight motion until they begin to set but are still creamy
- Add crème fraîche and continue to stir until the eggs reach your desired consistency
- Season with salt and pepper to taste
- Serve immediately, garnished with chopped chives

Vegetable Soup

Estimated Cook Time: 40 minutes

Necessary Ingredients: Assorted Vegetables (such as carrots, celery, potatoes), Ham, Vegetable Broth, Kale, Onion, Garlic, Herbs, Salt, Pepper

Description: Warm up with a comforting bowl of homemade vegetable soup. Simmer a medley of fresh veggies in a flavorful broth seasoned with aromatic herbs and spices for a nourishing and soothing meal that's perfect for any occasion!
- Chop all the vegetables into bite-sized pieces
- In a large pot, heat some olive oil over medium heat
- Add chopped onion, kale, and garlic to the pot and sauté until fragrant then seperate some kale for later use
- Add the chopped vegetables to the pot and cook for a few minutes, stirring occasionally
- Pour in the vegetable broth and bring to a boil
- Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let the soup simmer for about 30 minutes or until the vegetables are tender
- Season the soup with salt, pepper, and your favorite herbs
- Garnish with chopped ham and extra sautéed kale
- Serve hot and enjoy the comforting goodness of homemade vegetable soup!

Fluffy Pancakes

Estimated Cook Time: 15 minutes

Necessary Ingredients: Flour, Milk, Eggs, Baking Powder, Sugar, Butter, Maple Syrup (optional)

Description: Start your day with a stack of fluffy pancakes drizzled with sweet maple syrup. Whip up a simple batter, cook until golden brown, and enjoy these light and airy delights for breakfast or brunch!
- In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and a pinch of salt
- In another bowl, whisk together the milk, eggs, and melted butter
- Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. (tip: Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are okay)
- Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease with butter or cooking spray
- Pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake
- Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancakes and the edges look set, then flip and cook until golden brown on the other side
- Serve hot with a pat of butter and a drizzle of maple syrup and a variety of fruit, if desired. Enjoy your fluffy pancakes!

California Rolls

Estimated Cook Time: 45 minutes

Necessary Ingredients: Sushi Rice, Nori Seaweed, Imitation Crab Meat, Avocado, Cucumber, Sesame Seeds, Soy Sauce, Wasabi, Pickled Ginger

Description: Dive into the world of sushi with homemade California rolls!
- peel and cut avocado into thin slices and cucumber into matchstick size peices
- Lay a sheet of nori seaweed on a bamboo sushi mat, shiny side down
- Spread a thin layer of fresh sushi rice over the nori, leaving a small border along the edges
- Arrange avocado slices, cucumber sticks, and imitation crab meat in a line across the rice
- Sprinkle sesame seeds over the fillings
- Roll the sushi tightly using the bamboo mat, applying gentle pressure as you roll
- Slice the roll into bite-sized pieces using a sharp knife dipped in water to reduce friction
- Serve with soy sauce, wasabi paste, and pickled ginger. Enjoy your homemade California rolls!
