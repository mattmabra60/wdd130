What to bring?

-Rain jacket and rain pants (avoid ponchos)
-Quick-dry shirts and shorts
-Long-sleeve cotton shirt and pants for sun protection
-Light-weight cotton pant, nylon pant, or sarong
-Swim trunks for men, polypropylene long underwear if cold-natured
-Swim suits or quick-dry underwear, sport bras for women
-Quick-dry shorts and shirt (polypropylene if cold, cotton if hot)
-Two-piece swim suits for convenience
-Hat with a large brim and a quality strap
-Sunglasses with a strap
-Socks for hiking and sun protection
-Sandals or water shoes
-Comfortable camper wear (cotton dresses, skirts, or sarongs)
-Sleepwear or comfortable clothes for sleeping
-Water bottle
-Small daypack for hikes
-Moisturizing lotion
-Headlamp or flashlight
-Moleskin for blister prevention
-Waterproof sunscreen and lip balm
-Optional items: gloves, fleece jacket, towel, bandana, small pillow
-Hygiene products: toothpaste, toothbrush, tissue, nail clippers, feminine hygiene products, extra eyeglasses or contacts, biodegradable soap and shampoo
-Camera equipment
-Book, watercolors, journal, and pen
